The Problem

The hiring process is broken.
We fix your end of it.

Most job applications fail before a human ever reads them. Here's what's happening.

01

The Manual Edit Loop

You spend hours tweaking your CV for every role — changing keywords, reordering bullets, adjusting tone — while the job posting expires. There's no time and no clear signal of what actually matters.

02

The Silent Rejection

You never hear back — not even a rejection. Your application looks fine to you, but small misalignments in language, missing keywords, or weak phrasing quietly bury it before it reaches a hiring manager.

03

The ATS Black Hole

Over 75% of CVs are rejected by applicant tracking systems before a human sees them. ATS software scores your CV against the job description — and most people have no idea how they're being evaluated.

How it works

Three steps to a tailored application.

No setup, no account, no waiting. Drop in your CV and a job posting and get results instantly.

Step 01

Upload your CV

Drop your PDF and our parser extracts every section, bullet, skill, and role automatically. No copy-pasting, no reformatting — it reads exactly what you have.

Step 02

Paste the job

Paste the full job description or just drop in a URL — we'll fetch the page for you. The more detail, the better the analysis. LinkedIn URLs may need copy-pasting.

Step 03

Get your edge

Receive a fit score, rewritten bullet points, ATS keyword breakdown, skill-gap suggestions, a personalised cover letter, and a downloadable resume — all tailored to that specific role.

What you get

Everything you need to apply with confidence.

Six AI-powered outputs, generated in a single pass, in under a minute.

Fit Score

A 0–100 score showing how well your profile matches the role, with a colour-coded match label — Great, Fair, or Needs Work — so you know where you stand immediately.

ATS Keyword Analysis

See the top 8–10 keywords from the job description flagged as Strong, Weak, or Absent in your CV — with a note on whether each keyword is required or preferred by the employer.

CV Bullet Rewrites

Only the bullets that genuinely benefit from a change are rewritten. Language is sharpened to reflect the role's priorities, without inventing skills or inflating metrics.

Gap Suggestions

For each skill gap, you get a specific, buildable project idea relevant to your background — not generic advice to "take a course". Concrete things you can actually add to your portfolio.

Cover Letter

A focused 3-paragraph letter built from your actual CV achievements, referencing specific details from the job description. No stale openers, no fluff, no hallucinated skills.

Resume Editor & PDF

Your rewritten resume opens in a live editor — click any text to edit, then download as a clean, professional PDF. The tailored rewrites are already spliced in.
